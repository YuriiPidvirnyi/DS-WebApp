# Security Policy

We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability,
please report it to us privately.

# Reporting Process

1. Email us at info@dentalstory.ua with details
2. Include steps to reproduce the vulnerability
3. Allow us reasonable time to address the issue
4. We will keep you informed of our progress

# Out of Scope

- Social engineering attacks
- Phishing attacks
- Physical security issues
- DoS/DDoS attacks

Thank you for helping keep Dental Story and our users safe!
