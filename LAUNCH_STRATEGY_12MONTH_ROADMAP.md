## CONTINGENCY PLANNING

### Risk #1: Low Website Traffic
**Symptom:** <500 sessions/month in month 1-2
**Response:**
1. Audit website for technical issues (speed, mobile rendering)
2. Increase Google Local Services ad spend by 50%
3. Launch Facebook ads campaign earlier than planned
4. Check SEO optimization (keywords, meta descriptions)
5. Contact 10 past patients for reviews

### Risk #2: Low Booking Conversion Rate
**Symptom:** <10% conversion rate in month 1-2
**Response:**
1. Simplify appointment form (remove non-essential fields)
2. Add live chat support
3. Offer promotional incentive (20% off first visit)
4. A/B test different CTA button text/colors
5. Add trust signals (testimonials above fold)

### Risk #3: Negative Reviews
**Symptom:** Patient leaves 1-2 star review
**Response:**
1. Respond professionally within 24 hours
2. Offer to resolve issue directly
3. Follow up with patient after resolution
4. Document issue for process improvement
5. Train staff to prevent recurrence

### Risk #4: Competitor Launch
**Symptom:** New dental clinic opens in market
**Response:**
1. Differentiate on service quality, not price
2. Accelerate patient testimonial collection
3. Strengthen loyalty/referral programs
4. Consider limited-time promotional offer
5. Emphasize unique brand positioning (care-focused)
